# Data Science Jobs EDA

Exploratory data analysis on 9,355 data science job postings using PostgreSQL.

## Business Questions
1. What are the top 10 highest paying job titles?
2. How does experience level affect salary?
3. Which countries pay the most for data roles?
4. Remote vs Hybrid vs In-person — which pays more?
5. Which job categories are most in demand and highest paying?

## Key Findings
- Machine Learning & AI pays the most at $178k avg
- Senior roles dominate the market with 6,709 postings
- US pays significantly more than Europe (~$54k gap vs UK)
- In-person roles surprisingly pay more than remote by ~$10k
- Data Engineering is 2nd in volume with strong $146k avg salary

## Tools
- PostgreSQL
- TablePlus
- Dataset: Jobs in Data (Kaggle)
